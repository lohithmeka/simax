Simax Intelligent Governance Platform

Our platform deploys specialized AI driven solutions that act as intelligent building blocks for your workflow. Each solution is a self-contained product designed to automate the most time-consuming government workflows and deliver measurable results.

Data Intelligence & Management

Problem

Data in government is scattered across departments, buried in unstructured files, and siloed in incompatible systems, making it a challenge to access and use.

Solution

We provide a comprehensive suite of tools that unify, clean, and transform disparate data into a single, reliable source of truth.

  • Simax Scribe - Processes multilingual scanned PDFs, images, and handwritten notes, converting them into structured, usable data.

  • Simax Quanta - An AI-powered retrieval solution that finds any document instantly using natural language queries, such as "2019 pension scheme notification," without complex keyword searches.

Administrative Automation

Problem

Bureaucratic processes are slowed by repetitive, time-bound tasks and complex compliance-heavy documentation.

Solution

Our automation tools streamline these workflows, freeing officials to focus on strategic oversight and citizen services.

  • Simax RTI Accord - An automated query response system that instantly generates accurate, compliant responses to Right to Information queries.

  • Simax LAQ Accord - An intuitive query response system that prepares responses for legislative questions from elected representatives and flags any discrepancies against previously published or disseminated public data.

  • Simax Assure - A budget and financial tracking solution that monitors budget commitments and tracks real-time expenditures with built-in assurance monitoring.

Strategic Insight & Policy Support

Problem

Policy-making often reacts to problems instead of anticipating them.

Solution

We empower decision-makers with the foresight they need by combining AI analysis with real-time and historical data.

  • Simax Compass - A predictive policy modeling tool that simulates policy outcomes and gains actionable insights using department specific custom AI models and predictive analytics.

  • Simax Lexi - An AI-assisted legal intelligence solution that accelerates legal interpretation and ensures compliance with tools for rapid, side-by-side legal document comparisons.

  • Simax Memo - An automated note generation tool that creates structured, compliant bureaucratic notes with contextual tagging in minutes, reducing administrative overhead.
