Data Intelligence & Management

Transforming fragmented government data suitable for actionable intelligence

Key Products:

  • Simax Scribe - Processes multilingual scanned PDFs, images, and handwritten notes, converting them into structured, usable data.

  • Simax Quanta - An AI-powered retrieval solution that finds any document instantly using natural language queries, such as "2019 pension scheme notification," without complex keyword searches.

What

A comprehensive suite of tools that extract structured data from complex government documents in multiple languages and formats and unifies, cleans, and transforms fragmented government data into a single, reliable source of truth.

How

We use AI (HTR, OCR and NLP) to process multilingual PDFs, images, and handwritten notes, converting them into structured, usable data. Our tools standardize disparate data formats across departments and automatically tag and categorize documents for efficient retrieval. Our AI-powered retrieval solution finds documents instantly using natural language queries.

Why

Eliminates data silos, ensures consistency, and creates reliable foundations for decision-making across departments. by solving the challenge of scattered and inaccessible data. Reduces manual data entry by 90%, processes documents 10x faster, and enables instant information retrieval.

Strategic Insight & Policy Support

Predictive insights and custom models for strategic governance

Key Products:

  • Simax Compass - A predictive policy modeling tool that simulates policy outcomes and gains actionable insights using department specific custom AI models and predictive analytics.

  • Simax Lexi - An AI-assisted legal intelligence solution that accelerates legal interpretation and ensures compliance with tools for rapid, side-by-side legal document comparisons.

  • Simax Memo - An automated note generation tool that creates structured, compliant bureaucratic notes with contextual tagging in minutes, reducing administrative overhead.

What

Department-specific AI models that predict outcomes, simulate policies, and generate strategic insights.

How

We empower decision-makers by combining AI analysis through department specific LLM / SLM with real-time and historical data. This includes a predictive policy modeling tool, an AI-assisted legal intelligence solution for document comparisons, and an automated note generation tool.

Why

Transforms governance from being reactive to proactive by giving decision-makers the foresight to anticipate problems before they emerge.

Administrative Automation

Tailored solutions for specialized governance functions

Key Products:

  • Simax RTI Accord - An automated query response system that instantly generates accurate, compliant responses to Right to Information queries.

  • Simax LAQ Accord - An intuitive query response system that prepares responses for legislative questions from elected representatives and flags any discrepancies against previously published or disseminated public data.

  • Simax Assure - A budget and financial tracking solution that monitors budget commitments and tracks real-time expenditures with built-in assurance monitoring.

What

A set of automation tools that streamline bureaucratic processes and compliance-heavy workflows.

How

The system automates tasks such as instantly generating responses for Right to Information (RTI) queries and legislative questions (LAQs). It also tracks budget commitments and real-time expenditures with built-in assurance monitoring.

Why

It frees officials from repetitive, time-bound tasks, allowing them to focus on strategic oversight and citizen services.
